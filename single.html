Mobile Details

Microsoft Lumia 535 is a dual-SIM smartphone which features a 5-inch qHD display and comes with a 5-megapixel front facing shooter.

The smartphone features a 5-inch IPS LCD capacitive touchscreen with qHD (960x540 pixels) screen resolution which results in a pixel density of 220 PPI. The 5-inch screen also has Corning's Gorilla Glass 3 protection. Under the hood, it is powered by a 1.2GHz quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 200 processor and 1GB RAM. Further, the device has an internal storage capacity of 8GB which can be expanded up to 128GB via microSD card slot present on the device.

In the imaging depot, the smartphone comes with a 5-megapixel auto-focus rear camera with LED flash which can capture quality images and record videos. It also has a 5-megapixel front snapper which can click amazing selfies and make effective video calls.

The device is 8.8mm thick and weighs 146 grams. It sports a 3.5mm audio jack and comes with an FM Radio. Connectivity options include 3G, Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n, Bluetooth 4.0 LE and microUSB 2.0 port alongside twin SIM slots. The smartphone is powered by a Li-Ion 1905mAh battery which is expected to keep the device functioning throughout the day.

Lumia 535-The More you get the less you pay

This is the first complete Microsoft device launched recently. It is the latest, the cheapest and the best. The hardware and the software is all by Microsoft, so expect premium quality hardware and experience the superior software Windows Phone 8.1. Here is the review on it-

1) Design and Display- It has got that same lovely polycarbonate body with a glossy finish as seen on Lumia 730 orange. It feels really great to hold as the grip on the glossy part is good. The "Microsoft Mobile" embossed at the back gives it a decent brand value. It has got the dual sim capability. It is pretty thin but a little heavy as it weighs 146 gms. However, the 5 inch display fits perfect in hand. It is made for those who love a bigger screen and want to do more. The qHD 960x540 pixels on an IPS LCD screen provide good viewing angles and better resolution at this price point. It boasts a Scratch Resistant Glass(Corning Gorilla Glass 3). Various features like Brightness Control, High Brightness Mode and the trademark Sunlight Readability have been put in to make the experience more brighter, crisper and clear.

2) Performance- The 1.2 Ghz Snapdragon 200 quad core clubbed with 1GB RAM is a boomerang. It is very much able to give an ultra smooth performance without any stutters or lags. These were the ideal specifications people were dying for. Now it has got all you want at only 9k. Most of the games like Asphalt 8 play smooth. Multitasking was never so simple and beautiful as on this device. The various enhancements and changes in the software have facilitated a good experience overall. The only things to worry about are-
i) When you are playing music, you would see minute stutters while you go back to the main screen by pressing the back or the home button. The tiles stutter a bit but that's okay.
ii) If you fill up the main screen with hell lot of things the same thing will happen again as mentioned above.
iii) Some apps and games may take a bit extra time to open sometimes but they will run smooth. For example, the Xbox Games takes too long sometimes.
However, the rest is all fine except for negligible issues like minute lags or stutters. But for day-to-day activities, its surely not a disappointment.

3) Software- It comes preloaded with the new Windows 8.1 Update 1 Lumia Denim which brings with it a lot of camera stability improvements and much more. Windows 8.1 was a huge update. It changed the environment and optimized apps to run on a low end hardware. However, 1GB RAM in windows is like a 2GB in Android. With the Word Flow, Action Centre, Windows is now at par with IOS and Android. Only certain things need improvement like the IE, Play Store apps, etc but they will be soon at par as the Windows 10 arrives. Play Store has less apps but quality and required apps. So never say that the useful apps are not there. Still, the Play Store lacks in Google applications which are much needed. It has Office pre installed which is nice. Windows is not a resource hungry environment like Android and hence your phones will last longer unlike Android Phones which last for a year max.

4) Camera- It has got a 5MP primary and a secondary camera which is a good news for selfie lovers. The various camera applications like Lumia Camera, Lumia Panorama, Video Tuner, Blink, etc make your phone a DSLR. It captures amazing photographs in the low light, thanks to the LED Flash. It has got a few bugs, very small ones which would be fixed for sure.

5) Battery-The battery back up is really good now. It comes with 1905 maH battery which would last for a day surely. But, for frequent travellers I would not recommend it. It may be a plus point depending on your usage.

6) Storage- It has got 8GB Storage which is expandable to 128GB by micro sd card. So never worry about anything, play it your style.

Verdict- According to me, at 9k you are getting a quad core, 1GB RAM, 5MP primary and secondary camera, 5 inch qHD display and 1905 maH battery with WP 8.1. It also has Orientation Sensor, Proximity Sensor, Ambient Light Sensor and Accelerometer. It is a very good bargain considering the brand value and associated factors. If you love the WP environment, go for it if you can spend Rs 9000. I would give it a rating of 4.4 stars.
