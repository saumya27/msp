Samsung Galaxy Grand Prime Details

Samsung Galaxy Grand Prime is a selfie focussed dual-SIM smartphone powered by a Snapdragon 400 processor.

The smartphone features a 5-inch TFT capacitive touchscreen having qHD (960x540 pixels) screen resolution which results in a pixel density of 220 PPI. The device is 8.6mm thin and weighs 156 grams. Under the hood, it is powered by a quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 400 processor with clock speed of 1.2GHz coupled with 1GB RAM. The device also has an internal storage capacity of 8GB which can further be expanded up to 64GB using the microSD card slot present on the device.

If you are selfie crazy, the device is specially designed to fulfil your needs as it comes with a 5-megapixel front camera which allows you to not only take quality self-portraits but simultaneously make efficient video calls. It also has an 8-megapixel auto-focus rear camera which is capable of capturing quality images even in low-light situations and recording Full HD videos.

In terms of connectivity, the smartphone has 3G support alongside twin SIM slots, Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n, GPS with GLONASS, Bluetooth 4.0 and microUSB 2.0 port. It is fuelled by a 2600mAh battery which keeps the device functioning throughout the day.
