Mobile Details

The Galaxy S5 offers a world class viewing experience, top-notch imaging, and intuitive software features, along with a brilliant and ergonomic water-resistant design.

The Galaxy S5 sports a 5-inch Full HD (1920x1080) Super AMOLED display for vivid images with high contrast and deep blacks. Samsung's Local Contrast Enhancement technology helps the display adapt to various light conditions. It can get extremely bright so you can view everything easily even under direct sunlight, or go extremely low so you can read comfortably at night without straining your eyes. Adapt Display optimizes the viewing experience by adjusting the colour range, saturation and sharpness of the display based on whether you're watching a video, browsing the web, or going through your photos in the gallery.

The Galaxy S5 takes clear, high-resolution pictures thanks to its 16-megapixel rear camera, which features an ISOCELL sensor for impressive image capture in low light. Fast auto-focus means you will never miss those important moments, while Selective Focus lets you refocus on any part of an image. Users can record Full HD videos, download various camera modes, and also make video calls and take selfies using the 2.1-megapixel front-facing camera.

The Galaxy S5 comes equipped with a fingerprint sensor, which lets users keep their device secure, make payments on the web, and access private files and folders. A heart rate sensor measures your heart rate and feeds data into the inbuilt S Health app, which lets you track your daily fitness routines. For those with an active lifestylye, the Galaxy S5 is also water and dust-resistant for protection against the elements.

The octa-core Exynos 5422 processor and 2GB RAM offer blazing-fast performance without skipping a beat even during the most intensive tasks. There is 16GB of internal storage for installing all your favourite apps, and a microSD slot that supports up to 128GB cards means you will never run out of space for storing your photos, videos and music.

The Galaxy S5 runs Android 4.4 KitKat and includes a slew of intuitive features. Download Booster increases download speeds by combining a Wi-Fi and mobile data network, Ultra Power Saving Mode lets you extend battery life dramatically by switching the display to black and white and shutting off most functions on the phone. Users also have the best of Google's online services at their disposal, as well as thousands of apps to choose from to further enhance their smartphone experience.
