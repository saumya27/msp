HTC Desire 620G Details

Powered by a 1.7GHz oct-core processor, with 1GB RAM, 8-megapixel camera, the HTC Desire 620G dual-SIM is powered by a 1.7GHz oct-core processor with 1GB of RAM and is slotted in the mid-range price bracket. The device comes in a variety of colors : Marble White, Milkyway Gray, Saffron Gray, Santorini White, Tangerine White, Tuxedo Gray.

The HTC Desire 620G Dual Sim comes with a 5-inch Super LCD capacitive touchscreen with a resolution of 720x1280p which gives a pixel density of 294 PPI. Powered by a 1.7 GHz octa-core Cortex A-7 and armed with a MediaTek MT6592 with 1 GB RAM, the device gives super fast performance without any lags or stutters. There is also a Mali-450MP4 GPU chipset which gives the user a good-gaming experience. It runs on the Android 4.4 KitKat OS along with HTCs Sense 6 UI out of the box.

The device comes equipped with an internal storage memory of 8GB. There is also the option of expanding the memory via the microUSB port which gives you an extra 32GB storage space. The device sports an 8-megapixel rear camera with LED flash that gives you good quality images even in low-light conditions. There is also a 5-megapixel front camera which helps you take selfies and for making video calls. Furthermore, the video recording also lets you record high quality videos at 1080p.

The HTC Desire 620G Dual Sim gives you a lot of connectivity options such as, Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n, Bluetooth 4.0, GPS and a microUSB 2.0 port. The device is powered by a removable Li-Po 2,100mAh battery which lasts up to a day.
