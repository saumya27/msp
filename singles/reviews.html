  • Samsung Galaxy Grand 2 is a dual-SIM phablet from Samsung
  • It is the successor of the company's ultra-popular phone - Samsung Galaxy Grand
  • This phablet has a 5.2 inch HD TFT LCD Screen with HD (1280x720 pixels) resolution
