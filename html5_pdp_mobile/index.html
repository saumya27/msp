About the phone
    • Samsung Galaxy J7 is a dual-SIM smartphone that runs Android 5.1 Lollipop
    • The device has a 5.5-inch IPS LCD capacitive touch screen with a resolution of 267 PPI
    • It comes with a 13 MP primary camera with LED flash and a 5 MP secondary camera
    • The phone is powered by a 1.2 GHz octa-core processor with 1.5GB RAM
    • It has 8GB internal storage which can be expanded up to 64GB using a microSD card
    • Connectivity options include 4G, Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n, Bluetooth 4.0 and microUSB 2.0
    • The device is powered by Li-Ion 3000 mAh battery
