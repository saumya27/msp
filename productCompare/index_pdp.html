Apple iPhone 6 Details

One of the differentiating features of the iPhone is its overall experience, which for the first time is available on a device with a larger screen size. Relegated to the 3,5-inch form factor for the last three generations, the iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus are the first iOS devices to offer large screen real estate to users.

The iPhone 6 comes with a 4.7-inch LCD display with a pixel density of 326 PPI. The screen also has a Sapphire Crystal glass, which protects it from damages and stratches. The device also runs on the Apple iOS and the Apple A8 chipset with dual-core 1.4GHz Cyclone (ARM v8-based) processor. There is also a PowerVR GX6450 (quad-core graphics) for gaming.

This device comes with an 8-megapixel iSight rear camera. It has a large sensor and a big pixel size which allows more light to get in, resulting in low-light pictures. You can make high quality video calls using the HD front camera. The Backside Illumination Sensor (BIS) gives good quality images in low-light conditions.

iOS 8 adds some new features such as adding voice to text messages, choosing from third-party keyboards, track heart-rate and other fitness parameters, act on notifications without leaving an app, and sync the iPhone with the iPads and Macs. You could also browse through the 1,300,000 app options that are available in the App Store. iOS 8 is much more customizable when compared to earlier iterations, and you immediately get the feeling that the software ties in with the hardwares ability to redefine the iPhone line. Customizable keyboards, allowing you to install Fleksy or Swiftkey, are welcome additions that have long been requested by users.

Although there isnt much to differentiate the iPhone 6 from the 6 Plus at a hardware level other than the larger screen size, one noticeable omission on the smaller device is OIS, which is available on the 6 Plus. The reasoning for this is that the sensor was too large to fit into the body of the iPhone 6. While most users wont miss the feature on account of how good the iSight camera on the iPhone 6 is in and of itself, it would have been even better if OIS was also included.

Iphone 6-The Best of Apple

Iphone 6 is the best of Apple and the best of all smartphones considering its price tag, brand, and other features.
Let us analyse its pros and cons one by one through this review-

1) Design and Display- It comes with a smooth metal surface that seamlessly beats all its previous designs as it is better in every way. A redefined new Aluminium body with better positioning of buttons and a slimmer 7.1 mm body clubbed with lightness is the killer combo. It weighs only 129 gms and sports a new Retina HD display which is bigger and perfect- 4.7 inch display with 750 x 1334 pixels resulting in a 326 ppi pixel density. It has a shatterproof glass and oleophobic coating which protects it from accidental drops and damages. The LED Backlit IPS LCD display with enhanced Touch ID(Fingerprint Sensor) and the latest feature Display Zoom just seems perfect for a phone like this. It supports a nano sim with better speakers and sound as expected. So, overall its a pro.

2) Performance- The latest and the greatest Apple A8 chip combined with a powerful Dual-core 1.4 GHz Cyclone (ARM v8-based) with PowerVR GX6450 (quad-core graphics) clubbed with 1GB RAM for an Apple Device is top class. It is far better than a Snapdragon 801 quad core, Adreno 330 and 2GB RAM when compared with respect to benchmark scores. It is a performance beast and does not stutter or lag even once. It works blazing fast and smooth like butter with great software, animations and improved features. Hence, I rate it 5/5.

3) Software- It comes in built with iOS 8 which is obviously upgradable to iOS 8.1.3 and the future updates. It is almost similar to iOS 8 but has an improved action centre, Swype Keyboard, Optimized Siri, Apple pay and such other great features. The ringtones and notification tones are much better. The apps are well optimized to run the latest software. Hence, its surely a plus.

4) Camera- The new Iphone 6 has the 8MP primary camera, with dual LED flash and other camera features. It has a poor 1.2 MP secondary camera which is still good but not the best. However, the primary camera is certainly pretty good. It captures crisp and clear images with Full HD recording feature. Hence, I rate it 4.8/5.

5) Storage- It comes with inbuilt 16GB/32GB/64GB storage which is non expandable. Nowadays, if you go for a 16GB Model, you cannot just store everything as today, music, photos, apps are downloaded in bulk and need more space. Hence, the non expandable part is still a bad thing. Therefore, I give 3.5/5 stars.

6) Battery- This is the point where Apple has always lost some of it customers. It has a meagre Non-Removable 1810 maH battery which would max last a day. So, lazy people have to run for their chargers frequently and its a nightmare for travellers. However, it has a reversible connector which is a good feature. Hence, it is a must con.

Verdict-

Pros
1) Bigger and Better Display
2) Unique, Slim, Thin and improved design
3) A performance beast with hell lot of features
4) A well optimised software with great animations and apps
5) A mind blowing camera

Cons
1) A very very poor battery backup of 1810 maH
2) Non expandable storage-No support of micro SD card
3) A hefty price

Overall, I rate this phone 4.7/5 considering all its factors. It can be purchased for 42-45k online, however, if you have such a budget, then this phone will suit you well, but for middle class, its not a winner!
